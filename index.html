ABOUT
ME

Travels and photography, two passions that inspire me and are reflected in my projects. Culture in general and arts have always taken my attention that’s why the interdisciplinary nature of journalism convinced me to study it at the University of Buenos Aires in my home country Argentina. With the bachelor title in my hands I followed my other passion: travelling.

I putted my professional career on hold and bought a one-way ticket to New Zealand to live the adventure of travel anyway the wind blows. After such amazing landscapes (nature and urban) next to incredible persons, I felt the attraction of capturing these moments with a camera, awakening my passion for photography.

Many years and countries before, the wind bring me to Denmark where I decided to complement my acquired skills with a formal study in Multimedia Design at the Zealand University. Today I can proudly show my portfolio as content-creator and multimedia designer with skills in the range of video and image production; web design using languages as HTML, CSS and JavaScript; knowledge in graphic design and design thinking principles in order to control the whole creating process of the multimedia industry.

Take a look around! I’d love to hear what you think about my work, positive and negative feedback is really important to keep improving every day so and thanks for your time.
